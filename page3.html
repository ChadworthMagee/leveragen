Strategic Insights: Pathways to the U.S. Market

The "Buy" Approach (M&A)

Acquiring U.S. assets for immediate scale, manufacturing capacity, and market presence. Exemplified by Bora Pharmaceuticals.

The "Made in USA" Approach

Onshoring final production steps to mitigate trade risks and secure market access. Used by PharmaEssentia and StemCyte.

The "Innovate" Approach (R&D)

Leading with novel technology and validating it with premier U.S. institutions like the Mayo Clinic. A strategy used by JelloX Biotech.
